Bread omelette

  • INGREDIENTS
  • Egg - 2
  • bread slice - 2
  • Peanut butter - 4 Tbs
  • ketchup - 1 Tbs
  • salt - 1/2 Tsp

STEPS :

  1. Toast the bread slices
  2. Apply Peanut butter on bread slices
  3. Apply ketchup on top of bread
  4. Break the omelette, pour it in a bowl
  5. Add salt and beat the egg well
  6. pour the egg to the pan and once its became fluffy, add the toast on top
  7. fold it and have.

Pair it with :

  • Coffee
  • Cold Boost
  • Tea
